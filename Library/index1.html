Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our library.

How can I become a member?

You can become a member by visiting the library and filling out a membership application form. Please bring a valid ID and proof of address.

What are the library hours?

Our library hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday, 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Can I borrow e-books?

Yes, we offer a selection of e-books available for borrowing through our online platform. A valid library membership is required.

Do you have Wi-Fi access?

Yes, free Wi-Fi is available throughout the library for members and visitors.
